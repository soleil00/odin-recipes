Spaghetti Recipe

Description

A simple and delicious spaghetti recipe with homemade tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles.
  2. Brown the ground beef with onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce.
