Pizza Recipe

Description

A classic pizza recipe with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Add cheese and pepperoni.
  4. Bake in the oven until golden brown.
